Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

No Accounts or Tracking

The Lens is a stateless web application. We do not require accounts, we do not use tracking cookies, and we do not store your data in any database.

How Your Data is Processed

When you submit a question, it is sent securely to our third-party AI provider, Groq, to generate the philosophical perspectives. Groq may process and retain these inputs temporarily to provide the service and improve their models, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Do Not Submit Sensitive Information

Because your questions are processed by external AI systems, we strongly advise against submitting Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as real names, addresses, or sensitive medical/legal details. The Lens is designed for philosophical inquiry, not for storing personal data.
